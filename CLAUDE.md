# Fireworks Training Cookbook

Ready-to-run training recipes built on the [Fireworks Training SDK](https://github.com/stainless-sdks/fireworks-ai-python) (`fireworks.training.sdk`). Only `training/` is relevant -- ignore other top-level directories.

**Skill:** [`skills/training/`](skills/training/SKILL.md) — one skill covering both greenfield and recovery. Entry in `SKILL.md` maps tasks and error signals to specific reference files under `skills/training/references/`. The SDK repo points here; do not maintain a parallel skill there.
